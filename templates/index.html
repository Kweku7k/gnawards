About Us

GNMEA
Overview

Ghana nurses and midwife Excellence Awards is an initiative headlined by Golbal media Alliance (ETV,Yfm and happy fm) and also in partnership with UNSAG and GHATi.
1. It focuses on putting students on platforms and getting them recognized to the general public and copperative brands and individuals as well.
2. Its an award scheme for both degree nursing/midwives students and traning nurses/mideives
3. It awill also serve as a medium to bring all health students together from different parts of the country.
4. Though its an awards scheme, the platform creates a business activity as well for students to be recognized by sponsors or other brands out there. In this awards scheme you can be awinner but might not a brand ambassador or might not be rewarded by them due to the task preformed for them
4. Additionally its a platform seekng to bring brands(institutions) closer to their target audience to create a business.
5. Application is for free on our website
6. All students participants are advised to get a passport so incase if you should win the travel package the process would be easy (if they have or dont have you will be given a link to get one done the moment they are selected)

This awards is a business, lifestyle and academia awards

Requirements They are over 25 categories Some of them comes with requirements needed to endorse your skill talent or academic background.
Task Every Nominee gets a chanve to be interviewed on air live with sponsors or bloggers Based on category requirements students nominees works are publiced equally to create awareness 100% for their skill talent or business. Every Nominee for a period would have a chance to impress a brand or sponsor by performing a task or activity for a brand This will give students the chance to have a working relationship with the brand that gets to be impressed.

Benefits of all Nominees Publicity (on Air, websites, bloggers sites and socials) Business deals and prices(most be performed nominee on task associated with brands)

Awards and Benefits
1.Every winner of each category will be awarded a plaque and citation.
2.Highest number of votes will give students in any category a prize packages all under terms and conditions. -vacation trip.
3.Though its an awards we would equally appreciate second highest voters with an internal classy vacation trip. -electronic gadgets nad cash will also be awarded to category winners who will all reach the terms and conditions of their category.
This is the biggest nurses and midwives awards festival.
